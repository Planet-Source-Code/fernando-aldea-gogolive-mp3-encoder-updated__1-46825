GOGOLive MP3 Encoder
---------------------
GogoLive is the Fastest MP3 Encoder in Real Time.
Highly recommended for user with low speed PC. 
It has been proven in 486 at 166Mhz with good results.

Programmed by Fernando Aldea G.   (Valparaiso, Chile). 
e-mail: fernando_aldea@terra.cl



Before Execute:
  -Rename files:

   Gogo.ren   ------>   Gogo.dll
   GOGOfunc2.ren --->   GOGOfunc2.dll
   
  -Move its at System directory (Windows/System in Win9x) or .EXE directory.

Feature:
  -Record from you Sound Card directly (analog mode ovbiosly).
  -Make MP3 on-Fly (by pipe line).
  -Multiples Options encode: 
      32-320 Kbps, 8000-44100 Hz, 
      Stereo/Joint Stereo/Ms-Stereo/Dual/Mono
      Psy-Acoustic mode, Multiple CPU (if available), MMX. etc
  -Control CD Incorporated (sync).
  -Monitor Input Level
